What I value

How I approach design

  • Start with the problem

    I begin by understanding users, systems, and context before jumping into solutions. Research, data, and usability principles guide my decisions — not assumptions or visual preferences.

  • Design for complexity

    I work at a systems level, connecting flows, features, and constraints to create experiences that are clear, scalable, and consistent across products.

  • Work through collaboration

    I value curiosity over judgement and communicate design rationale clearly, building trust with product, engineering, and business teams to move decisions forward together.

  • Keep learning

    I'm currently deepening my background in psychology to strengthen how I understand behaviour, communicate intent, and design with greater empathy.
