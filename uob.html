UOB Infinity — Corporate Digital Banking Platform

Overview

About the project

UOB Infinity is a corporate digital banking platform supporting complex financial workflows, including cross-border payments and corporate transactions across multiple markets. As UX Designer, I was embedded in an agile delivery team, working across multiple product streams to incrementally improve and evolve the user experience.

Project 01

UOB Pay / UOB Send

Cross-border payment workflows

Improved cross-border payment workflows within the corporate banking platform, focusing on making key transaction steps clearer and more intuitive for users. The work involved refining interaction patterns and aligning the experience with regulatory and system constraints required for financial transactions.

Project 02

Virtual Account Sub-Account (VASA)

The VASA initiative introduced the ability for corporate users to create and manage sub-accounts within their organisations. I designed the experience for creating and managing sub-accounts within corporate organisations.

The challenge was to represent complex account hierarchies in a way that remained clear and manageable for users performing financial administration tasks.

Project 03

Boatsize Creative Changes

Supported rapid product updates and short-turnaround initiatives where design changes were required quickly due to business or operational needs. This involved collaborating closely with product and engineering teams to deliver feasible UX solutions while maintaining usability and design consistency.

Project 04

Fraud Detection & Risk Monitoring Dashboard

Designed an internal dashboard used by backend teams to monitor and manage risk, fraud, and security within the banking platform. The focus was on presenting complex operational data in a clear and actionable way to support faster decision-making and system oversight.
