About Me

With 8+ years in design, I create smart and thoughtful digital products.

I'm a Singapore-based UX designer working on complex digital products in the banking space. I partner with product, engineering, and business teams to turn requirements, constraints, and ideas into experiences that people can actually use.

With a background in branding and marketing, I bring a systems-level perspective to product design — connecting user needs, business goals, and delivery realities.

I use systems thinking and experimentation to define the right problems and shape purposeful, insight-driven solutions.

More about me

  • 01

    Start with the problem

    I begin by understanding users, systems, and context before jumping into solutions. Research and usability principles guide my decisions — not assumptions.

  • 02

    Design for complexity

    I work at a systems level, connecting flows, features, and constraints to create experiences that are clear, scalable, and consistent across products.

  • 03

    Work through collaboration

    I value curiosity over judgement and communicate design rationale clearly, building trust with product, engineering, and business teams.

  • 04

    Keep learning

    I'm currently deepening my background in psychology to strengthen how I understand behaviour and design with greater empathy.

How I Work

Four disciplines.
One purpose.

The way I approach every design challenge — from discovery to delivery — built around clarity, empathy, and execution.

Experience Design

Turning complex workflows into clear, usable journeys from end to end — balancing user behaviour, business goals, and technical constraints.

Product Thinking

Connecting user insight, business goals, and execution end to end — ensuring design decisions move the right metrics.

Strategy

Framing the right problems before exploring solutions — helping teams align on what to build and why before diving into how.

Systems Thinking

Creating cohesive patterns and journeys that support products as they grow — designing components and flows that scale.
